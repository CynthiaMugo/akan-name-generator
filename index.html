Akan Name Generator

Have you ever wondered what your name would be in another culture? In Ghanaian Akan tradition, names aren't just given at random—they're deeply meaningful and tied to the day of the week a person is born. Each day has a specific name for males and females, often reflecting personality traits, social expectations, and spiritual significance.
This Akan Name Generator is a fun and easy way to tap into a beautiful part of Ghanaian culture. Just pop in your birth date, and we’ll show you your Akan name—based on the day of the week you were born. You’ll also get a quick peek at what your name means. It’s a cool way to learn something new about yourself and maybe even find a name that totally matches your vibe!
